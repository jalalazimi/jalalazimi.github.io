Momenta

Capture your moments as pictures with sound.

  • Co-Founder, Design & Development
  • 2013 – 2014

During my internship at Door2Door in Berlin, I joined Alex, Jose and Kirsty as a co-founder of Momenta. My focus was on all things web, allowing me to work on both the design and development of the product.

Momenta is a simple yet beautiful way to capture and remember life’s special moments. Through the powerful combination of pictures and sound, it allows you to share a special moment of your life with the people you care about.

Landing Page

Previously to the launch of the iPhone App, I designed and coded the landing page for Momenta.

Momenta for the Web

Sharing and embedding moments outside of the App.

Apple ended up adding a similar feature to iOS in September 2015: Live Photos. It was — together with our growing user-base — a welcome validation of our concept.

Goodbye

We eventually sunsetted Momenta to move on to other projects. Nonetheless I’ve learned a tremendous amount about community building and working remotely in a team.

Being 17 years old at the time, I was able to gain a huge amount of insight from my teammates on designing products, building them, and eventually exposing them to the real world.
